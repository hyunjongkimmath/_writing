
\begin{definition} \label{definition:preimage_of_an_open_subset_of_a_ringed_spaces_under_a_morphism_of_ringed_spaces}
Let $(X, \mathcal{O}_X)$ and $(Y, \mathcal{O}_Y)$ be \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:ringed_space}{ringed spaces}, and let 
$$f : (X, \mathcal{O}_X) \to (Y, \mathcal{O}_Y)$$ 
be a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:morphism_of_ringed_spaces}{morphism of ringed spaces}. For any open subset $V \subseteq Y$, we define the \hldef{preimage of $V$ under $f$} to be the ringed space whose underlying topological space is the open subset
$$\hlin{f^{-1}(V) := \{ x \in X \mid f(x) \in V \} \subseteq X}$$
and whose sheaf of rings is the restriction of $\calO_X$ to $f^{-1}(V)$. 
\end{definition}
