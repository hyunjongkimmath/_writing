    Let $S$ be a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:representable_by_schemes_for_a_morphism_of_presheaves_on_Sch_S}{scheme}. A property $\mathcal{P}$ of morphisms of schemes over $S$ is said to be \hldef{preserved under base change} if whenever a morphism $f: X \to Y$ over $S$ has $\mathcal{P}$, the base change $f': X_{S'} \to Y_{S'}$ over any morphism $S' \to S$ has $\mathcal{P}$.
