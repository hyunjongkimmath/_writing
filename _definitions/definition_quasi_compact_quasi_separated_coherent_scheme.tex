\begin{definition} \label{definition:quasi_compact_quasi_separated_coherent_scheme}
A \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:scheme}{scheme} is called \hldef{qcqs} (or \hldef{coherent}) if it is both \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:quasi_compact_scheme}{quasi-compact} and \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:quasi_separated_morphism_of_schemes}{quasi-separated}.
\end{definition}
