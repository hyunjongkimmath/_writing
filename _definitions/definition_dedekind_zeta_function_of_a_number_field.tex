\begin{definition} \label{definition:dedekind_zeta_function_of_a_number_field}
Let $K$ be a number field, and let $s \in \mathbb{C}$ with $\Re(s) > 1$. The \hldef{Dedekind zeta function of $K$} is defined as
$$\zeta_K(s) = \sum_{\mathfrak{a} \neq (0)} \frac{1}{N_{K/\mathbb{Q}}(\mathfrak{a})^{s}},$$
where $\mathfrak{a}$ runs over all nonzero ideals of $\mathcal{O}_K$.
