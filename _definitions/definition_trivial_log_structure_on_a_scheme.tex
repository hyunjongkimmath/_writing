\begin{definition} \label{definition:trivial_log_structure_on_a_scheme}
Let $X$ be a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:scheme}{scheme}. 
The \hldef{trivial log structure on $X$} is the \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:pre_logarithmic_structure_and_logarithmic_structure_on_a_scheme_and_log_scheme}{log structure} defined by setting $M = \mathcal{O}_X^*$ and $\alpha: \mathcal{O}_X^* \hookrightarrow \mathcal{O}_X$ to be the natural inclusion.
\end{definition}
