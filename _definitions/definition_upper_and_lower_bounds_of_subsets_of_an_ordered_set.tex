\begin{definition}[Upper and lower bounds in an ordered set] \label{definition:upper_and_lower_bounds_of_subsets_of_an_ordered_set}
Let $(X,\leq)$ be an \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:partially_ordered_set}{ordered set} and let $S \subseteq X$ be a subset.
\begin{enumerate}
  \item An element $u \in X$ is called an \hldef{upper bound} of $S$ (in $X$) if $s \leq u$ for all $s \in S$.
  \item An element $\ell \in X$ is called a \hldef{lower bound} of $S$ (in $X$) if $\ell \leq s$ for all $s \in S$.
\end{enumerate}
\end{definition}
