Let $M$ be a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:C_k_manifold}{smooth $n$-dimensional manifold}, and let $T_pM$ denote the \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:tangent_space_of_a_topological_manifold_at_a_point}{tangent space} at a point $p \in M$. A \hl{metric tensor on $M$} is a smooth symmetric $(0,2)$-tensor field
$$ g: TM \times TM \to \mathbb{R}, \quad (X,Y) \mapsto g_p(X_p, Y_p), $$
that is non-degenerate at every point $p \in M$.
\end{definition}
