\begin{definition} \label{definition:etale_cohomology_group_of_a_scheme_with_coefficients_in_an_abelian_group}
Let $X$ be a scheme. Let $A$ be an abelian group and write $\underline{A}$ for the \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:constant_sheaf_on_a_site_with_sheafification}{constant sheaf} on \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:small_etale_site_of_a_scheme}{$X_{\mathrm{\acute{e}t}}$} associated to $A$. In this case, the \hldef{Ã©tale cohomology groups of $X$ with coefficients in $A$} are defined as 
$$\hlin{H^i_{\mathrm{\acute{e}t}}(X, A) := H^i_{\mathrm{\acute{e}t}}(X, \underline{A}).}$$
(\Cref{definition:etale_cohomology_of_a_sheaf_of_abelian_groups_on_the_small_etale_site_of_a_schem}); here $\underline{A}$ is regarded as a module of the constant sheaf $\bbZ$ on $X_{\et}$. 
\end{definition}
