\begin{definition}[Discrete topological space] \label{definition:discrete_topological_space_of_a_set}
Let $X$ be a set. The \hldef{discrete topology on $X$} is the topology \hl{$\mathcal{T}_{\mathrm{disc}}$} defined by
\[
\mathcal{T}_{\mathrm{disc}} \;:=\; \{ U : U \subseteq X \},
\]
i.e., every subset of $X$ is open. The pair $(X, \mathcal{T}_{\mathrm{disc}})$ is called a \hldef{discrete topological space}.
\end{definition}
