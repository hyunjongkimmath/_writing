\begin{definition}[Field of real numbers] \label{definition:field_of_real_numbers}
A \hldef{field of real numbers} is a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:ordered_field_complete_ordered_field}{complete ordered field} characterized up to unique isomorphism of ordered fields; as such we may refer to such a field as \emph{the} field of real numbers. We denote such a field by \hl{$\mathbb{R}$}.
\end{definition}
