\begin{definition} \label{definition:hyperbolic_plane_over_a_commutative_ring}
Let $R$ be a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:commutative_ring}{commutative ring}.
A \hldef{hyperbolic plane} is a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:quadratic_formm_on_a_module_over_a_commutative_ring}{quadratic module} \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:isometry_of_quadratic_modules_over_a_commutative_ring}{isometric} to \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:product_of_modules_of_rings}{$R^2$} eqiupped with the quadratic form given by $q(x,y) = xy$.
\end{definition}
