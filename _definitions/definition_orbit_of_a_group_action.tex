
\begin{definition}[Orbit] \label{definition:orbit_of_a_group_action}
Let $(G,\cdot)$ be a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:group}{group} \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:left_and_right_group_actions_on_a_set}{acting on} a set $X$, with action denoted by $g \cdot x$. For $x \in X$, the \hldef{orbit of $x$} under the action of $G$ is
$$\hlin{G \cdot x = \{ g \cdot x : g \in G \}}.$$
\end{definition}
