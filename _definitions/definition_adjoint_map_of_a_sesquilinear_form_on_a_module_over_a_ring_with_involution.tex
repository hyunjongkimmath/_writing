\begin{definition} \label{definition:adjoint_map_of_a_sesquilinear_form_on_a_module_over_a_ring_with_involution}
Let $(R, \sigma)$ be a ring with involution and $M$ a left $R$-module equipped with a sesquilinear form $\phi: M \times M \to R$. The \hldef{adjoint map of $\phi$} is the morphism of $R$-modules
$$ \hat{\phi}: M \to M^\vee, \quad u \mapsto (v \mapsto \phi(v, u)) $$
where $M^\vee = \operatorname{Hom}_R(M, R)_\sigma$ is the \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:twisted_dual_module_of_a_module_over_a_ring_with_involution}{twisted dual module}.
\end{definition}
