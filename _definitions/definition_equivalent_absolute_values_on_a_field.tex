\begin{definition} \label{definition:equivalent_absolute_values_on_a_field}
Two \hyperrefIfExists{definition:absolute_value_on_a_field}{absolute values}\CrefIfExists{definition:absolute_value_on_a_field} $|\cdot|_1$ and $|\cdot|_2$ on a field $F$ are \hldef{equivalent} if there exists a positive real number $c > 0$ such that
\[
|\cdot|_1 = |\cdot|_2^c.
\]
\end{definition}
