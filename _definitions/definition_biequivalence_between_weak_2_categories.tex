\begin{definition} \label{definition:biequivalence_between_weak_2_categories}
Let $F, G: \mathcal{C} \to \mathcal{D}$ be \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:pseudofunctor_between_weak_2_categories}{pseudofunctors} between \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:bicategory}{weak 2-categories (bicategories)}.
A \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:pseudofunctor_between_weak_2_categories}{pseudofunctor} $F: \mathcal{C} \to \mathcal{D}$ is a \hldef{biequivalence} if there exists a pseudofunctor $G: \mathcal{D} \to \mathcal{C}$ and \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:pseudonatural_transformation_between_pseudofunctors_between_weak_2_categories}{pseudonatural transformations} $\eta: id_{\mathcal{C}} \Rightarrow G \circ F$ and $\varepsilon: F \circ G \Rightarrow id_{\mathcal{D}}$ that are internal equivalences in the respective bicategories of functors.

\end{definition}
