
\begin{definition} \label{definition:simple_ring}
A \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:ring}{not necessarily commutative ring} $R$ is called \hldef{simple} if $R \neq 0$ and the only \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:ideal_of_a_ring}{two-sided ideals of $R$} are $\{0\}$ and $R$ itself.
\end{definition}
