\begin{definition} \label{definition:geometrically_connected_scheme_over_a_field}
If $X$ is a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:scheme_over_a_scheme}{scheme over} a field $k$, we say $X$ is \hldef{geometrically connected over $k$} if the structure morphism $X \to \operatorname{Spec}(k)$ has geometrically connected fibers; equivalently, if $X \times_k \operatorname{Spec}(\bar{k})$ is connected.
\end{definition}
