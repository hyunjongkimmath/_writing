\begin{definition}[Convex Cone] \label{definition:convex_cone_in_a_finite_dimensional_real_vector_space}
Let $V$ be a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:basis_of_a_vector_space_over_a_field}{finite-dimensional} real \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:vector_space_over_a_field}{vector space}.
A subset $\sigma \subset V$ is a \hldef{convex cone} if for any $x, y \in \sigma$ and any $\lambda, \mu \geq 0$, we have $\lambda x + \mu y \in \sigma$.
\end{definition}
