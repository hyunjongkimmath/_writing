
\begin{definition}[Quotient Representation] \label{definition:quotient_representation_of_a_representation_of_a_group_on_a_left_module_on_a_ring}
Let $(G, \cdot)$, $R$, $M$, and $\rho$ be as above, and let $N \subseteq M$ be an $R$-submodule which is stable under the representation $\rho$ (i.e., $\rho(g)(N) \subseteq N$ for all $g \in G$).

The \hldef{quotient representation of $G$} on the quotient $R$-module $M/N$ is the homomorphism
$$\rho_{M/N} : G \to \operatorname{Aut}_R(M/N),$$
defined by
$$\rho_{M/N}(g)(m + N) = \rho(g)(m) + N \quad \text{for all } m \in M, g \in G.$$
\end{definition}
