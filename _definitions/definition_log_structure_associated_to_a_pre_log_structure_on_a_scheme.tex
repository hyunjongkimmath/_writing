\begin{definition} \label{definition:log_structure_associated_to_a_pre_log_structure_on_a_scheme}
Let $X$ be a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:scheme}{scheme}. 
    Let $P$ be a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:monoid}{monoid} and let $\beta: P \to \Gamma(X, \mathcal{O}_X)$ be a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:homomorphism_of_monoids}{homomorphism of monoids}. This induces a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:constant_sheaf_on_a_site_with_sheafification}{constant sheaf} of monoids $\underline{P}$ on $X$. The \hldef{log structure associated to a pre-log structure} determined by $\beta$ is the \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:pushout_of_objects_under_an_object_in_a_category}{pushout} of $\underline{P}$ and $\mathcal{O}_X^*$ over $\beta^{-1}(\mathcal{O}_X^*)$ in the category of sheaves of monoids, often used to define chart-based log structures.
\end{definition}
