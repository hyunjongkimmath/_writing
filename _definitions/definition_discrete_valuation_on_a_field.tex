\begin{definition}[Discrete valuation] \label{definition:discrete_valuation_on_a_field}
Let $(K, v)$ be a \hyperrefIfExists{definition:valuation_on_a_field}{valued field}. The valuation $v$ is called \hldef{discrete} if the value group $\Gamma = v(K^\times)$ is isomorphic, as an ordered group, to $\mathbb{Z}$ (the integers with the usual order).
\end{definition}
