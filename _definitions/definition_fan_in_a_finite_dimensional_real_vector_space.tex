Let $N$ be a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{defintion:lattice_over_a_commutative_ring}{lattice} of rank $n$, and let $N_{\mathbb{R}} = N \otimes_{\mathbb{Z}} \mathbb{R}$.
A \hldef{fan in $N_{\mathbb{R}}$} is a finite collection $\Sigma$ of \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:strongly_convex_cone_in_a_finite_dimensional_real_vector_space}{strongly convex} \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:rational_polyhedral_cone_with_respect_to_a_lattice_in_a_finite_dimensional_real_vector_space}{rational polyhedral cones} in $N_{\mathbb{R}}$ such that:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Every \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:face_of_convex_cone_in_a_finite_dimensional_real_vector_space}{face} of a cone in $\Sigma$ is also a cone in $\Sigma$.
    \item The intersection of any two cones in $\Sigma$ is a face of each.
\end{enumerate}
\end{definition}
