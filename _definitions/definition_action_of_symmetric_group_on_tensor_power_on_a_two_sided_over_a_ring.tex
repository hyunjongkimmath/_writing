\begin{definition} \label{definition:action_of_symmetric_group_on_tensor_power_on_a_two_sided_over_a_ring}
Let $n \in \mathbb{N}$. The \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:symmetric_group_on_a_set}{symmetric group} on $n$ letters is denoted by $\mathfrak{S}_n$.  
For a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:module_of_a_ring}{two-sided module} $M$ over a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:ring}{(not necessarily commutative) ring}, this group \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:left_and_right_group_actions_on_a_set}{acts (on the right)} on \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:tensor_product_of_bimodules_of_rings}{the tensor power $M^{\otimes_R n}$} by permutation of the tensor factors: on the pure tensors, the action is given by
$$ (x_1 \otimes \cdots \otimes x_n) \cdot \sigma := x_{\sigma(1)} \otimes \cdots \otimes x_{\sigma(n)}, \quad \sigma \in \mathfrak{S}_n, \; x_i \in M $$
and the action is extended linearly.
\end{definition}
