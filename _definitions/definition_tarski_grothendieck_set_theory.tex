\begin{definition} \label{definition:tarski_grothendieck_set_theory}
\hldef{Tarski--Grothendieck set theory}, denoted by \hl{$\mathsf{TG}$}, is the theory consisting of the axioms of \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:zermelo_fraenkel_set_theory}{$\mathsf{ZFC}$} together with \hldef{Tarski's Axiom of Universes}:
$$ \hlin{ \forall x \, \exists U \, (U \text{ is a Grothendieck universe} \land x \in U) } $$
\end{definition}
