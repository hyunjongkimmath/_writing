
\begin{definition} \label{definition:holomorphic_morphism_of_sheaves_of_O_X_modules_on_a_complex_analytic_space}
Let \(X\) be a complex analytic space, and let \(\mathcal{F}\) and \(\mathcal{G}\) be sheaves of \(\mathcal{O}_X\)-modules (or more generally, coherent analytic sheaves) on \(X\). A \hldef{holomorphic morphism} $\mathcal{F} \to \mathcal{G}$ is simply a $\mathcal{O}_X$-module morphism.
\end{definition}
