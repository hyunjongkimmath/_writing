A \hldef{pseudo-Riemannian manifold} is a pair $(M,g)$ where $M$ is a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:C_k_manifold}{smooth manifold} and $g$ is a non-degenerate symmetric $(0,2)$-tensor field on $M$. The \hldef{signature of $g$} is the pair $(p,q)$, where $p$ and $q$ are the numbers of positive and negative eigenvalues of $g_p$ in any local coordinate basis.
\end{definition}
