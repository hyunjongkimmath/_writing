\begin{definition}[Non-degenerate quadratic form]  \label{definition:nondegenerate_quadratic_form_on_a_module_over_an_involution_ring}
Let $R$ be a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:involution_on_a_ring}{ring with involution}. Let $q: M \to R$ be a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:quadratic_form_over_a_ring_with_involution}{quadratic form}. 
The quadratic form $q: M \to R$ is non-degenerate if for every $x \in M$, the map $y \mapsto \langle x, y \rangle_R$ is zero only if $x = 0$.
\end{definition}
