\begin{definition}
    \begin{enumerate}
        \item Let $M$ be a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:complex_analytic_manifold}{complex manifold} with or without boundary. A \hldef{K\"ahler metric on $M$} is a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:hermitian_metric_on_a_smooth_manifold_of_even_real_dimension}{Hermitian metric} whose associated fundamental $2$-form \hl{$\omega$}, called the \hldef{Kähler form}, extends smoothly to all of $M$ (in case that $M$ is with boundary), and is closed, i.e. 
        \[
        \omega(X,Y) = h(JX, Y), \quad d\omega = 0,
        \]
        where \( J \) is the complex structure on \( M \).
