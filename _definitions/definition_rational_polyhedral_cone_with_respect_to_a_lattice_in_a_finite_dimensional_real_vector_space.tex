\begin{definition}[Rational Cone] \label{definition:rational_polyhedral_cone_with_respect_to_a_lattice_in_a_finite_dimensional_real_vector_space}
Let $V$ be a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:basis_of_a_vector_space_over_a_field}{finite-dimensional} real \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:vector_space_over_a_field}{vector space}.
Let $N$ be a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{defintion:lattice_over_a_commutative_ring}{lattice} in $V$.
A \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:polyhedral_cone_in_a_finite_dimensional_real_vector_space}{polyhedral cone} $\sigma \subset V$ is called \hldef{rational (with respect to $N$)} if it can be generated by vectors $v_1, \ldots, v_k \in N$.
\end{definition}
