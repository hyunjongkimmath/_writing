\begin{definition} \label{definition:geometric_fiber_of_a_morphism_of_schemes_at_a_geometric_point}
Let $f: X \to Y$ be a morphism of schemes. 
For any \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:geometric_point_of_a_scheme}{geometric point} $\bar{y}: \operatorname{Spec}(\Omega) \to Y$, the \hldef{geometric fiber of $f$ at $\bar{y}$}, denoted \hl{$X_{\bar{y}}$}, is the \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:base_change_of_a_morphism_in_a_category_by_a_morphism}{base change}:
$$X_{\bar{y}} := X \times_Y \operatorname{Spec}(\Omega)$$
Note that this depends on the choice of the geometric point $\bar{y}$, not just on the image point $y \in Y$.
\end{definition}
