\begin{definition} \label{definition:support_of_a_weil_divisor_on_a_scheme}
Let $X$ be an \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:scheme}{scheme} and let $D = \sum n_i Y_i$ be a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:prime_and_weil_divisor_on_a_scheme}{Weil divisor} on $X$. 
    The \hldef{support of a divisor $D$}, denoted by \hl{$\operatorname{Supp}(D)$}, is the union of the prime divisors $Y_i$ for which $n_i \neq 0$.
\end{definition}
