\begin{definition}[Subspace topology and subspace] \label{definition:subspace_of_a_topological_space}
Let $(X, \mathcal{T})$ be a topological space and $Y \subseteq X$ a subset. The topology induced on $Y$ by $\mathcal{T}$, called the \hldef{subspace topology}, is defined as
$$\hlin{\mathcal{T}_Y \;:=\; \{ U \cap Y : U \in \mathcal{T} \}.}$$
The pair $(Y, \mathcal{T}_Y)$ is called a \hldef{subspace of $(X, \mathcal{T})$}.
\end{definition}
