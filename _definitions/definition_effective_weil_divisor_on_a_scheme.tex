\begin{definition} \label{definition:effective_weil_divisor_on_a_scheme}
Let $X$ be an \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:scheme}{scheme} and let $D = \sum n_i Y_i$ be a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:prime_and_weil_divisor_on_a_scheme}{divisor} on $X$. 
    We say that $D$ is an \hldef{effective divisor} if $n_i \geq 0$ for all $i$. 
\end{definition}
