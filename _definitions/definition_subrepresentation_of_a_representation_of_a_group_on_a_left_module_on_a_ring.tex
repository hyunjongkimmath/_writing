
\begin{definition}[Subrepresentation of a Representation] \label{definition:subrepresentation_of_a_representation_of_a_group_on_a_left_module_on_a_ring}
Let $(G, \cdot)$ be a group, $R$ be a ring with unity, and $M$ be a left $R$-module. Suppose 
$$\rho : G \to \operatorname{Aut}_R(M)$$
is a representation of $G$ on $M$. 

A \hldef{subrepresentation of $\rho$} is a pair $(N, \rho|_N)$ where $N \subseteq M$ is an $R$-submodule of $M$ such that 
$$\rho(g)(N) \subseteq N \quad \text{for all } g \in G,$$
and $\rho|_N : G \to \operatorname{Aut}_R(N)$ is defined by restricting the action of $G$ on $M$ to $N$.
\end{definition}
