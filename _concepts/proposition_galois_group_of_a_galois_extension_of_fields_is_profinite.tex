\begin{proposition}[Krull topology characterization] \label{proposition:galois_group_of_a_galois_extension_of_fields_is_profinite}
For a (possibly infinite) Galois extension $L/K$, the Galois group $G = \operatorname{Gal}(L/K)$ is a profinite group, i.e. a compact, Hausdorff, totally disconnected topological group isomorphic to an inverse limit of finite groups.
\end{proposition}
