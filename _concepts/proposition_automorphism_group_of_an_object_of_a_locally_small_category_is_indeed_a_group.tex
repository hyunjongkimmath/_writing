\begin{proposition} \label{proposition:automorphism_group_of_an_object_of_a_locally_small_category_is_indeed_a_group}
Let $\mathcal{C}$ be a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:locally_small_category}{locally small category} and $X$ be an object of $\mathcal{C}$.
The \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:automorphism_of_an_object_of_a_category_automorphism_group_of_an_object_of_a_locally_small_category}{automorphism group} $\operatorname{Aut}_{\mathcal{C}}(X)$ indeed forms a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:group}{group} under composition. The identity element is the identity morphism $\operatorname{id}_X$, and the inverse of an element $f$ is its inverse morphism $f^{-1}$.
\end{proposition}
