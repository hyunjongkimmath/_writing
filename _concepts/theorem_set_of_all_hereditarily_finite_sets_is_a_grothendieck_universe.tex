\begin{theorem} \label{theorem:set_of_all_hereditarily_finite_sets_is_a_grothendieck_universe}
    The set $V_\omega$ of all \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:hereditarily_finite_sets}{hereditarily finite sets} is a \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:grothendieck_universe}{Grothendieck universe}. Furthermore, $V_\omega$ is initial in the sense that it is contained in every Grothendieck universe (that may exist with respect to the chosen system of axioms containing \CrefAndHyperrefIfExist{definition:zermelo_fraenkel_set_theory}{ZF}); more precisely, it is the unique minimal Grothendieck universe under the inclusion relation $\subseteq$.
\end{theorem}
